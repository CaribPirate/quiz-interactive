April 30, 2015

by Jenna Cosgrove

Creativity Featured The Creative Mind

How creative are you? Take our creative IQ test to find out!

In the past week, how many different activities have you participated in (different sports, social activities, cultural outings, anything not related to work or normal family duties)

When you have to complete a logical task such as bookkeeping, are you able to maintain a clear focus until the task is finished?

Do you often feel like a square peg in a round hole?

How often do you visit museums and art galleries?

A friend calls you—she has a spare ticket to a concert tonight. It’s a style of music you don’t care about and you’ve never heard of the artist. It’s cold out and you're in your PJs. Do you go?

Would you consider yourself aggressive?

What word most accurately describes your parents?

How many times have you caught yourself daydreaming today?

Watch the short video above about the Reddit feature called The Button. What is your first impression when you watch it?

Have you participated in a physical activity for fun (not for weight loss/fitness/anything with a physical outcome) in the past week?

If a child was playing with a lego set next to you, would you want to join in?

When a creative solution needs to be found for a problem, is it best to work alone or brainstorm in a group?

You were on a small plane that crashed in the desert. There are 3 other survivors. You have managed to salvage 4 things from the wreck - which will be the most useful to your survival?

When you have a new idea, what is your first instinct?

You can take only one of the following items on a 15-hour flight with you. Which do you choose?
